Who We Are

India's Trusted Logistics Partner Since 1994

Shubham Logistic Services is a professionally managed, process-driven logistics company headquartered in Hyderabad, with a growing presence across India's key commercial and industrial cities.

With over 30 years of operational experience and a fleet of 60+ vehicles, we specialise in the safe, efficient, and compliant transportation of containerised, over-dimensional, temperature-sensitive, and high-value cargo across all major trade corridors.
