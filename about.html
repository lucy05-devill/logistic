Our Story

Built on Reliability. Grown on Trust.

Shubham Logistic Services was founded in 1994 with a clear purpose: to deliver cargo with the reliability, discipline, and care that businesses depend on. What began as a road transportation service in Hyderabad has grown into a pan-India logistics company, handling some of the country's most demanding cargo — from temperature-controlled pharmaceuticals to over-dimensional industrial machinery.

Today, we operate across India's major commercial and port cities — Hyderabad, Mumbai, Chennai, Delhi, Pune, Vizag, Bangalore, and Gujarat — with our fleet covering all key national highways, port corridors, and industrial trade routes. We serve importers, exporters, freight forwarders, and supply chain companies as a trusted ground execution partner.

1994 — Founded in Hyderabad

Started as a road transportation service with a commitment to reliability and on-time delivery.

2000s — Fleet Expansion

Expanded into specialised cargo — ODC, reefer, and air-suspension transport to serve diverse industries.

2010s — Pan-India Reach

Opened offices in Mumbai and extended operations to all major port cities and trade corridors.

Today — 60+ Fleet, 9 Cities

A fully process-driven logistics operation with GPS monitoring, verified personnel, and SOP governance.
