Our Advantages

Seven Reasons Clients Choose SLS

30+ Years of Proven Experience

Established in 1994, we have built deep operational knowledge across India's ports, ICDs, industrial zones, and highway corridors. Our experience means fewer surprises, faster problem-solving, and better outcomes for your cargo.

Pan-India Fleet of 60+ Vehicles

Air-suspension trucks, reefer containers, flatbeds, low-beds, and standard trailers — covering all major corridors from Gujarat to Vizag, Delhi to Chennai. A fleet built for every cargo type.

GPS-Enabled Real-Time Monitoring

Every vehicle is GPS-tracked. Our centralised operations team maintains live visibility across all active consignments, ensuring route discipline, on-time performance, and immediate response to deviations.

SOP-Governed Compliance

Structured, documented operations covering transport security, cargo handling, seal verification, personnel screening, and incident response — fully aligned with multinational logistics standards.

Background-Verified Personnel

All drivers and field staff are background-verified and trained for the specific cargo types they handle — from reefer transport to high-value air suspension cargo. Safety is never compromised.

Port & Corridor Network

Operational presence across JNPT, Vizag, Chennai, Mundra, Kandla, and Krishnapatnam — with established working relationships with freight forwarders, customs brokers, and shipping agents.

Expanding City Network

Offices and operations across Hyderabad, Mumbai, Chennai, Delhi, Pune, Vizag, Bangalore, and Gujarat — with fleet coverage spanning all of India's key industrial and trade corridors.

24-Hour Response Commitment

Every enquiry receives a tailored response within 24 hours. Our operations team is reachable Monday through Saturday, 9 AM to 6 PM — with emergency coordination available for critical consignments.
